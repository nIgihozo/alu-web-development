Guidelines

The Bible is a remarkable fountain: the more one draws and drinks of it, the more it stimulates thirst. One can never come away from it sated. It is an inexhaustible well. Therefore, it is impossible for a Christian to be sufficiently armed against the spirit of lies and error except with the divine weapon of the Word of God

Hint

I have so much to do that I shall spend the first three hours in prayer. It is not necessary, indeed it is not even possible, to work and to toil, and to labor from early until late without intermission. To be always full of cares and always worried; to study and strive and labor, but with such a disposition of mind that no time is left for prayer or for God; this is not good.

Word

Be a sinner, and let your sins be strong (sin boldly), but let your trust in Christ be stronger, and rejoice in Christ who is the victor over sin, death, and the world. We will commit sins while we are here, for this life is not a place where justice resides. We, however, says Peter (2. Peter 3:13) are looking forward to a new heaven and a new earth where justice will reign. Don't forget to pray

More Info

Faith is a living, daring confidence in God's grace, so sure and certain that a man could stake his life on it a thousand times. This confidence in God's grace and knowledge of it makes men glad and bold and happy in dealing with God and all His creatures. And this is the work of the Holy Ghost in faith. Hence a man is ready and glad, without compulsion, to do good to everyone, to serve everyone, to suffer everything, in love and praise to God who has shown him this grace.
